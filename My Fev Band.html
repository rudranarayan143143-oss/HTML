Sanam band

SANAM is a popular Mumbai-based Indian pop-rock band formed in 2010
(initially as The SQS Project), known for revitalizing old Bollywood classics
with modern, acoustic arrangements and producing original music. The
quartet—Sanam Puri, Samar Puri, Venky S, and Keshav Dhanraj—is recognized
as one of India's biggest independent YouTube music artists.
